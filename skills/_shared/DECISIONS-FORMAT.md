# DECISIONS.md — entry format (single source)

> Append-only — never edit a past entry; supersede it with a new one.

## D<NNN> — <short title>   (<date>)
**Context:** <what forced a choice>
**Decision:** <what was chosen>
**Alternatives:** <the genuine options not taken>
**Why:** <the trade-off reasoning>
**Status:** accepted | superseded by D<NNN>

## When to record a Decision — all three must hold
1. **Hard to reverse** — committing the method to this is costly to change later.
2. **Surprising without context** — a future reader, or the AI executing the skill, will wonder "why was it done this way?"
3. **The result of a real trade-off** — there were genuine alternatives and one was chosen for specific reasons.

If any of the three is missing, skip it. Most exchanges do not produce a Decision Record, and that is correct.
